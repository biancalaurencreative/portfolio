Art Basel Rubell Museum / Le Basque

 

Miami has Evolved into One of the Most Beautiful Cities in the World!

Modern Architecture in Miami brings glamour, fun, and material excess that began to flourish during the mid-century era. It was the playground for the jet-set, rat-pack age and is now making its remarkable revival. Today, no other city is comparable to Miami, which offers a vast diversity of cultural communities, museums, venues, architecture, and stunning hotels along the oceanfront and Biscayne Bay. It has also become a mega set in the culinary scene and fashion.

Miami’s newest thriving community in Allapattah has become a cultural icon with magnificent museums such as the Rubell Museum and Superblue Museum. With Miami’s incredible skylines and rich multicultural people and scene, it is no wonder that Miami has been breaking records in the US and International Tourism.

The new Miami Beach Convention Center has become the centerpiece of iconic venues, museums, and hotels just steps and minutes away. It is the must-see magnificent Art Basel exhibition.

EVOLVE Miami illustrates the finest in South Florida’s hospitality and offers authentic experiences
highlighting the essentials in venues, culinary, events, and lifestyle.
